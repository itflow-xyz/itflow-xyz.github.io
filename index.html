What is software?

A software is a group of programs which can be executed by a computer. There are many kinds of software, for example operative systems, applications (mobile apps), video-games and also some websites are runned by custom software. Everything you can not touch on a computer is software.

What is maintenance?

Like for cars, software also requires maintenance. Many softwares are built using standard pieces of code. Those pieces of code are public and some are given from the productor of base softwares.
Often happens that the productor of standard codes releases some updates, to improve their software or to fix some issues, after that a new version is released is good practice to check that everything works also with the new version and if something is going wrong fix it.

What areas do we work in?

We like to understand what our clients do, we want to learn about his market, his products, his people and his processes to help him make the best choice.
We are oriented to management systems and automation tools.

What is process automation?

When people work together there are some processes, many of those are recurrent and similar every time. Those processes often are boring, they may waste a lot of time and some time produce errors. Nobody is perfect!
Automating processes will reduce the errors, the boring jobs that have to be done by the people and the costs.
A complete win!
