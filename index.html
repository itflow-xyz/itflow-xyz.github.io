What is software?

Basically, a bunch of programs that can be executed by a computer. There are many kinds of them, such as operating systems, web-mobile applications, video-games and others.
In other words, anything that can not be physically touched on a computer, is a software.

What maintenance consists of?

Similarly to cars, softwares requires maintenance.
Is necessary to understand the need of implementing good and regular maintenance to every type of softwares.
Sooner or later, producers of standard codes release new updates with the purpose of improving their software’s quality or fixing some issues.
Therefore, maintenance is a good practice to keep a software healthy, by ensuring that the system still works properly after updates.

Which is our aim?

Our aim is to fully understand the needs of our clients. Through a specific analysis of their company, their target market, procedures and products.
Furthermore, we truly believe in delivering personal and unique solutions to each customer. Nevertheless, we are oriented to implement custom management systems and automation tools.

What is process automation?

Process automation consists of using technology to automate repetitive business procedures. Henceforth, it’s possible to avoid basic human mistakes together with better resource optimization. Surely a complete win!
